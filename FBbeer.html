Hand-Crafted Beers

  • Honey Wheat

    IBU 19 ABV 5.3

    This is our most popular and easy to drink ale. Honey Wheat is an American wheat ale with 30 pounds of honey added to the brew. Honey Wheat is lightly hopped, slightly malty yet well balanced, with a clean finish.

  • Ed's IPA

    IBU 65 ABV 6.0

    This English pale ale is made with premium malt, three English hop varieties, and a special British yeast culture. Fruity yeast aromas mix with the floral hops to greet your nose as you raise a pint of this brew. The bitterness is assertive, yet it is crisp, dry, and clean. This beer is named for our long time patron, Ed Richardson. This beer was his favorite!

  • Green Chili Ale

    IBU 22 ABV 5.3

    This cult classic has 50 lbs of Anaheim peppers added during fermentation to create a great chili flavor. We also add 3.0 pounds of Jalapeno peppers along with 1.5 pounds of Serrano peppers to give it a touch of heat. Basic 2-row base malt plus a small amount of crystal malt give this beer its color. Try it with tomato juice for a “liquid enchilada”!

  • Brown Ale

    IBU 12 ABV 5.7

    Our Brown is a British Style brown. It has a roasted nutty flavor complimented by a great malt sweetness that comes from a local sourced pilsner malt and five different specialty malts.

  • Irish Red Ale

    IBU 12 ABV 4.8

    A rich reddish ale brewed with five different specialty malts which give this ale its color, flavor and aroma. Slightly roasted and malt forward flavors dominate with light hopping to provide some balance with little bitterness or hop flavors in the finish. Flavorful, crisp, easy drinking beer.

  • Katy Trail Ale

    IBU 47 ABV 5.8

    This popular American pale ale is amber colored, with a balance of malt and hop flavor. American Cascade hops add a subtle citrus aroma.

  • Oil Change Stout

    IBU 13 ABV 5.4

    Black as diesel oil, Oil Change is big in roasty chocolate flavors. Lots of flaked oats gives this stout a velvety smoothness. Oil Change is carbonated with nitrogen to give it a thick creamy head.

  • Mango IPA

    IBU 15 ABV 5.2

    A refreshingly light American styled IPA flavored with fresh mango juice. Mosaic hops dominate the flavor with subtle citrus notes from late kettle and whirlpool additions. The mango juice adds sweetness and flavor notes that compliment the hop profile. An easy drinking brew that is done in the IPA style without the big hop footprint typical of the style.

  • Mystic Pale Ale

    IBU 40 ABV 5.0

    Easy drinking pale ale with a nicely balanced profile. An experimental hop variety was used for bitterness and flavor notes which present as subtle tropical fruit. Little to no bitterness with slight malt notes in the finish. A nice refreshing brew to compliment our hot summer weather.
